Practical Information

Where to stay?

If you come by car or rent a car, we recommend you to stay in nice characteristic place called Turist Garden (https://www.turistgarden.com/) in Töcksfors. This place is just across the Swedish border and only 10 minutes drive from Ørje. It's a small place so don't wait too long with booking!

If you don't have a car, the most practical option is to stay in the Scandic Brennemoen hotel in Mysen (https://www.scandichotels.no/hotell/norge/mysen/scandic-brennemoen). It is a comfortable hotel and it has a great train and bus connection with Oslo. We will organise transportation to and from Mysen both on Friday and Saturday.

If you feel like camping, you're very welcome to set up your tent on Gustav's farm. This is where on Friday night the welcome barbecue and Sunday morning the goodbye brunch take place. On Saturday we will organise transportation to and from the main wedding venue. If you would really like to camp but cannot bring a tent, ask us -- we have a few spare tents that we're happy to make available! To come to Ørje, either take the train to Mysen or the bus to Töcksfors and we will make sure someone will be there to pick you up!
