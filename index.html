Dear Family and Friends,

It is with insurmountable joy that we invite you to our wedding this summer!

Almost 10 years ago we walked into each other at a breakfast table in Moldova. Life together has been an incredibly exciting, ever-enticing ride ever since.

It became time to celebrate this with everyone who is dear to us!

For this we invite you to a boat-party in Ørje, the beautiful place where Filippa grew up.
